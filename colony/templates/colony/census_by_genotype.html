Census View, sorted by genotype

This is a list of all cages in the colony, sorted by genotype. Cages may be listed multiple times if they contain a mixture of genotypes.

The genotype of each mouse is listed and can be of the following types:

  • pure WT: typically purchased from Jackson
  • pure stock: a mouse with a single transgene, all of whose ancestors were WT or had the same transgene

Each breeding cage is one of the following types:

  • Outcross: pure stock breeding with pure WT
  • Incross: pure stock breeding with pure stock of the same genotype
  • Cross: pure stock breeding with pure stock of a different genotype

Each non-breeding cage is one of the following types:

  • Pure stock: contains the offspring of an outcross or incross
  • Progeny: contains the offspring of a cross

For example, a Halo +/- mouse would be pure stock if it were derived from a Halo x WT outcross, but would be progeny if it were derived from a Nr5a x Halo cross and did not inherit the Nr5a.
